About Us

Every child deserves a chance to thrive

Grace of Light for Orphans (GLO) is a registered non-profit organization registered in Dowa District and Social Welfare is a compassionate and dedicated organization committed to improving the lives of orphaned, street kids, women and vulnerable children. We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Our team consists of passionate individuals, including social workers, educators, healthcare professionals, and volunteers, all united by the common goal of providing hope, love, and support to those who need it most.

Our Mission

The mission of Grace of Light for Orphans is to provide holistic care and support to orphans, women, and the most vulnerable children. We are committed to meeting their fundamental needs including food, shelter, clothing, and medical care while also offering access to quality education, emotional support, and life skills training. Through a nurturing and compassionate environment, we aim to empower every child to grow into a self-reliant, responsible, and contributing member of society. Our mission is to restore hope, create opportunities, and build brighter futures for those who need it most.

Our Vision

At Grace of Light for Orphans, we envision a world where every orphaned child is embraced with love, empowered through education, and inspired with hope. We strive to be a beacon of compassion and a catalyst for positive change, ensuring that every child we serve grows into a confident, self-reliant, and contributing member of society. Our vision is a future where no child is left behind where kindness, opportunity, and hope light the way for every child to thrive.

Core Values

Our core values are the guiding principles that shape our organization and drive our commitment to serving orphaned and vulnerable children. They reflect who we are, what we stand for, and how we approach our mission.
  • Compassion: We act with kindness, empathy, and understanding, prioritizing the emotional, psychological, and physical well-being of every child in our care.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and the highest ethical standards in all our actions, ensuring trust with children, communities, and partners.
  • Respect: We value the dignity, worth, and uniqueness of every child, recognizing their rights and treating them with fairness and humanity.
  • Empowerment: We equip children and guardians with the tools, skills, and opportunities they need to become self-reliant and reach their full potential.
  • Inclusivity: We foster an environment where every child feels accepted, valued, and celebrated, promoting diversity and equality at all levels.
  • Education: We believe education is the foundation for transformation and prioritize access to quality learning and lifelong personal development.
  • Community: We work hand-in-hand with families, local leaders, and communities to build a strong support system for the well-being of orphaned and vulnerable children.
  • Sustainability: We design programs that are resilient and long-lasting, ensuring that positive change endures across generations.
  • Hope: We inspire optimism, encouraging children to dream, believe in themselves, and pursue a brighter future.
  • Accountability: We remain responsible to the children we serve, our partners, donors, and communities, ensuring careful stewardship of resources and unwavering commitment to our mission.
